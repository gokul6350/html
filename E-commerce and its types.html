E-Commerce and Its Types



What Is E-Commerce?

E-Commerce reffers to any form of business transaction conducted onlne.
Ecommerce, also knownas electronic commerce or internet commerce, reffers the buying and selling of goods or survices using internet, and the transfer of money and data to execuite these transaction.

Different types of Ecommerce commerce are:-

There are 6 basic form types of e-commerce
  • (B2B) Business-to-Business
  • (B2C) Business-to-Consumer
  • (C2C) Consumer-to-Consumer
  • (C2B) Customer-to-Business
  • (B2A) Business-to-Administration
  • (C2A) Customer-to-Administration
All of them represent different purchasing dynamic.
